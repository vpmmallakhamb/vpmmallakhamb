More Than A Team

OUR Story

Just as cricket is played in every alley, Mallakhamb too must reach every corner.

This thought was the spark that ignited a beautiful journey. In 2016, our coach and alumni, Shri Mukesh Velonde, started a summer camp at Vidyamandir school with a simple vision: to keep the heritage of Mallakhamb alive in the very place that introduced him to it. Supported by the late Bhau Thakur, Dr. Vasundhara Natekar, and Vidya Satam, the VPM Mallakhamb movement officially began.

Over the last decade, through rigorous practice, competitive excellence, and the integration of modern tech like onemallakhamb.in for scoring, we have built a distinct culture. We celebrate traditions, festivals, and triumphs together. Today, VPM's Vidyamandir Dahisar is not just a club with elite State and National athletes—it is a family.

Our Journey

2016

The foundation is laid with the first Mallakhamb summer camp classes at Vidyamandir School, Dahisar.

2017 & 2018

Our athletes begin capturing district medals. Mrugank Pathare, Manthan Mirlekar, and Tarun Mallya become the first VPM boys selected for the State Championship in Alibaug.

2019

Hardika Shinde and Palak Churi make history as our first girls selected for States in Pune. VPM also successfully hosts the inaugural Balambhatdada Deodhar Trophy.

2020 & 2021

Despite the global lockdown halting ground practice, online fitness classes keep the fire burning. The hard work pays off in 2021 when Mrugank, Hardika, and Palak are selected for the Nationals in Ujjain, representing Maharashtra.

2022 & 2023

VPM athletes compete in the first-ever Khelo India Youth Games to feature Mallakhamb. Back home, the club installs an indoor frame for advanced rope practice and proudly hosts the DSO Zonal Selections.

2026

Mrugank created onemallakhamb.in, a web application for scoring and online points display at mallakhamb competitions.

The Journey Continues...

And the journey is going on with many medallists at IOA's National Games, Khelo India Youth Games, Khelo India University Games, Federation Nationals, School Games Nationals, All India University Games, Khelo India Beach Games, etc.
